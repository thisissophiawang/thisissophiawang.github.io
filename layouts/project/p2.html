Mental Talk

Project overview

A mental health support application that offers users a safe space for medical wellness and connects them with therapists through a user-friendly interface.

Persona

Jessica
Role: University Student

20Y/O Female

"I feel so overwhelmed with deadlines and don't know how to cope with all this pressure."

NEEDS:
  • A safe space to express emotions privately
  • Tools to gain clarity on her stressors
  • Connection with others facing similar challenges
FRUSTRATIONS:
  • Overwhelming academic pressure
  • Social anxiety making it difficult to seek help in person
  • Feeling isolated and alone in her struggles
PAIN POINTS:
  • Difficulty managing multiple deadlines
  • Lack of accessible mental health resources
  • Hesitation to take the first step toward professional support

Key features

Mental Talk combines several key features to create a holistic mental health support system:
  • Forum: Users can share their experiences and support one another in a safe and open environment.
  • Online Consultations: Users can easily book consultations with licensed therapists, ensuring access to professional help when needed.
  • Home Page: The home page displays upcoming appointment times and details about the assigned therapist, keeping users informed and organized.
  • Consultation Page: Users can browse different therapists' availability, specialties, and professional backgrounds to choose the right fit for their needs.
  • Resource Page: This section offers a variety of educational articles on mental health topics, helping users gain knowledge and insights.
  • Profile Page: Users can view their services, booked doctors, appointments, and available coupons, providing a personalized experience and easy access to their information.

Design decisions

We included a short and simple headline:

"Your safe space for medical wellness and support"

So new users can quickly understand the app's purpose. This clear message reassures users that Mental Talk is dedicated to providing a secure and supportive environment for their mental health needs.
